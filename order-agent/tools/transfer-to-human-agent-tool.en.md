Transfer complex or sensitive order issues to human customer service
**Applicable Scenarios**:
- Modification operations after order payment (address modification, cancellation, order modification/merge)
- Logistics anomalies for shipped orders (loss, delay, abnormality)
- After-sales service (returns, exchanges, warranty claims)
- Financial issues (invoices, payment errors, price negotiation)
- Business requirements (bulk purchasing, samples, customization, dropshipping)
- Product support (user manual)
**Inapplicable Scenarios**:
- Simple queries for unpaid orders, routine order status queries
- Address modification for unpaid orders, order cancellation, order merge
- Routine order status queries
- Operations that can be completed through self-service
